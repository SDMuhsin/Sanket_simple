AUGMENTED REALITY

The cards have changed, and Argumated Reality is getting more real everyday. To ensure the smooth transition into the world of AR, we are offering you Hands on workshop on AR Technology. The workshop will be started off with basics of game development using Unity engine and then progress into Android studio and Vuforia SDK, for image tracking and 3D shooting using green mat. Introduction to C# programming for scripting player data and UI connection. PREREQUISITES Participants should bring there own laptop with the necessary softwares pre installed (which will be informed upon registration).
